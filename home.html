About Susan Chan

Born in Malaysia and residing in the United States, Susan started her journey in Chinese Painting over 10 years ago. Under the tutelage of Mr. Paul Hau, she now focuses on the Pomo (潑墨) style. Her works have been exhibited in the US, China, and Taiwan, including major venues such as the Liaoning National Museum (2018), Chiang Kai-Shek National Memorial Hall, and Dr. Sun Yat-Sen Memorial Hall (2016).

Susan has published two art albums: The Passion and Path of Poetry and Art and Alluring Coloration. Her exhibitions include the Hou Beiren Art Museum in Kunshan, China; Chiang Kai-Shek National Memorial Hall; and Silicon Valley.

As an active member of the art community, Susan is the president of the American Society for the Advancement of Chinese Arts (ASACA). She teaches traditional Chinese painting and Splash Ink at Los Altos Community Services and Newark Adult Education Center. She also serves as the principal of North Valley Chinese School.
