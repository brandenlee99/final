Independent Facilitator Support

Our mission is simple: to bridge the gap between underserved communities and the services they need to thrive. We specialize in helping clients navigate the complexities of the Self-Determination Program (SDP), ensuring that every individual, regardless of language, culture, or background, has the tools and support to access the services that promote independence, choice, and personal growth.
